Tips & Tricks

•Learn the patterns of the pickaxes.
•Always watch the pickaxes nearest to you to avoid getting cornered.
•Use your diamond ores wisely to gain advantage against the pickaxes.
•Most importantly, Get gud.

How To Play:

You play as Rockman collecting ores along the maze.
Your goal is to collect all gold ores while being chased by the pickaxes.
Use the diamond ores to make the pickaxes weak and destroy them.
Have Fun and Good Luck !
